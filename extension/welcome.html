Aura

Rich Presence for the web.

Aura shows what you're watching, listening to, or browsing on your Discord profile — automatically. Works across YouTube, Netflix, Spotify, Twitch and more.

Setup in 2 minutes

  1. Open the Discord Developer Portal and create a new Application.
  2. Go to the Bot tab → Reset Token → copy the token.
  3. Copy the Application ID from General Information (optional, only for images).
  4. Invite the bot to any server you're in.
  5. Click the Aura icon, paste both values, hit Save & Connect.
  6. Browse a supported site — your status updates automatically.

What Aura can do

  • Auto-detect activity on 27+ websites
  • Custom activity override (Playing / Watching / Listening / Competing)
  • Per-site toggles with search
  • Auto-idle when AFK
  • Skip incognito tabs for privacy
  • Master on/off switch
  • Daily time-tracked counter
  • Export & import settings
  • English, Português and Español
